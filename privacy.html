Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

1. Introduction

At Lisaany, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what limited information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use our website, application, and services (the "Services"). We are committed to keeping the data we hold to an absolute minimum.

2. Information We Collect

We deliberately collect as little personal information as possible. Specifically:

Email address. We collect your email address so you can create an account, sign in, and so we can contact you about your account and the Services. This is the only personal detail we ask you for.

We may also store basic, non-sensitive information needed for the Services to work — for example, your learning progress, lesson completion, and preferences. We do not collect sensitive personal information, and we never ask for more than we need.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account.
  • To let you sign in and save your learning progress.
  • To contact you about your account, your subscription, or important updates to the Services.

We do not use your information for any purpose beyond operating and improving the Services.

4. We Never Sell or Share Your Email

We will never sell, rent, or share your email address — or any of your personal information — with any third party for marketing or any other purpose. Your email stays with us and is used only as described in this policy.

The only exception is where we are legally required to disclose information by a valid order from a competent authority.

5. Payments

All payments are processed securely by our payment provider, Stripe. When you make a purchase, your payment card details are entered and handled directly by Stripe — we never see or store your full card information on our servers.

Stripe processes your payment data in accordance with its own privacy policy and terms. You can review Stripe's privacy practices at stripe.com/privacy.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your account information is stored securely with our infrastructure providers. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

7. Cookies & Local Storage

We use only the cookies and local browser storage necessary for the Services to function — for example, to keep you signed in and remember your progress and preferences. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

If you are under the age of 18, you should use the Services only with the consent and involvement of a parent or legal guardian. We do not knowingly collect more information than necessary from anyone.

9. Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, or to close your account, at any time. To make a request, simply contact us at the email below.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of the Services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us at admin@lisaany.com.
