Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Can I switch between plans? expand_more
Yes — upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. No penalties.
What happens to my progress if I cancel? expand_more
Your progress is always saved to your account. If you cancel, you keep full access until the end of your paid period. Your data is never deleted.
How do I book a tutor session? expand_more
Premium subscribers see a "Book a Session" button inside the app. It opens the Calendly calendar — pick your time and a Google Meet link is sent to your email automatically.
Are sessions 1-on-1 or group? expand_more
Group sessions (maximum 15 students) are included in Premium. This keeps quality high and cost affordable for everyone. 1-on-1 sessions may be available as a future add-on.
Is there a free trial? expand_more
Module 1 of both the Tajweed and Arabic Conversation paths is completely free — no credit card required. Only upgrade when you're ready to unlock everything.
